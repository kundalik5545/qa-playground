## 9. 💡 Key Insights & Recommendations

### ✅ Strengths

1. **Excellent bounce rate (30%)** — Users are engaged and exploring multiple pages per visit.
2. **Strong session depth** — Average 7m 50s visit duration is well above average for tool/practice sites.
3. **AI-driven discovery is working** — ChatGPT and Gemini are sending meaningful traffic; this is a modern and valuable channel.
4. **Global reach** — 10+ countries visiting with a strong India + USA + Singapore core.
5. **Practice section is highly engaging** — `/practice` pages drive a large volume of multi-page journeys.

### ⚠️ Areas to Watch / Improve

1. **Traffic concentration spike (Mar 25)** — Determine what caused the spike and try to replicate it (social post? link share?). If it was a one-time event, plan for sustained acquisition.
2. **Bing > Google** — Invest time in Google SEO. The gap suggests Google rankings can be improved (internal linking, schema markup, page titles).
3. **Mobile is only 5%** — While expected for a QA tool, ensure mobile experience is at least functional for users who do browse on mobile.
4. **Journey drop from Step 1 → Step 2 is 54%** — More than half of entrants leave after the first page. Improving CTAs, internal navigation links, and "what to try next" prompts on landing/entry pages could increase this.
5. **`/signup` appears in Step 3 journeys (6 users)** — Some users are converting to accounts. Track this conversion funnel more closely and consider making sign-up more prominent for engaged users.
6. **`/bank` section is popular** — Consider expanding banking simulator features (transactions, transfers, more accounts) as this area drives significant traffic.
7. **Blog/Learn content** (`/blog/`, `/learn/`) is appearing in journeys — double down on SEO content creation to drive more organic discovery.
